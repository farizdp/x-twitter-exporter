About

X Exporter Viewer

Browse and categorize your exported Twitter/X Likes and Bookmarks.

How to use

1. Export your tweets using twitter_likes_exporter_v5.js or the browser extension.
2. Load the exported .json or .csv file here.
3. Browse the timeline, categorize tweets manually or with LLM auto-categorization.
4. Export/import your category data to preserve it across sessions.

Keyboard shortcuts

/ — Focus search
j / k — Navigate tweets
Esc — Close modals
